# Context

## The problem
Long URLs are hard to share in chat, print, and social posts, and they reveal nothing about
how often a link is used.

## The opportunity
A fast, dependable shortener with basic analytics covers the everyday need without the bloat
of a marketing suite.

## Product thesis
Make sharing a link effortless and let owners see whether anyone clicked.

## Non-goals
| Non-goal | Status |
| --- | --- |
| Custom vanity domains | deferred |
| Team workspaces and roles beyond owner | out |
