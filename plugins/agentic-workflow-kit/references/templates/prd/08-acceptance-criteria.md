# Acceptance criteria

*Grouped, ID'd ship checklist. Each group picks a short PREFIX (1-3 letters). Each criterion
is PREFIX-n and is designated `[ship blocker]` or `[target]`. `plan-track` maps stories to
these IDs.*

## <Group name> (PREFIX `<X>`)

| # | Criterion | Designation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **X-1** | <observable, testable statement of done.> | **[ship blocker]** |
| **X-2** | <observable, testable statement of done.> | **[target]** |

## Ship-blocker summary
All `[ship blocker]` criteria must be met before the product ships. `[target]` criteria may
be deferred when a documented workaround exists.
